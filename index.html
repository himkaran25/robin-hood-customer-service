How to Contact Robinhood Support

Struggling with your Robinhood account? Here's your complete guide to getting in touch with Robinhood support quickly and effectively.

1. In-App Support (Best Option)

The fastest way to reach Robinhood support is through the app:

  • Open the Robinhood app.
  • Tap the account icon (bottom right corner).
  • Select Help > Contact Us.
  • Describe your issue and request a callback or email.

2. Use the Web Platform

If you're on a desktop, you can also contact Robinhood via the website:

  • Visit: robinhood.com/contact
  • Log into your account.
  • Use the chatbot or request direct support.

3. Request a Phone Call

Robinhood offers a callback option:

Note: Robinhood does not have a direct phone line you can call. You must request a callback through the app or web interface.

4. Email Support

You can send an email from the app or desktop platform. Replies usually take 24-48 hours.

5. Social Media

Try reaching out via their official handles:

  • Twitter: @RobinhoodApp
  • Facebook: Robinhood

6. FAQs and Community

Before reaching out, browse their Help Center. Many issues are covered in detail.
